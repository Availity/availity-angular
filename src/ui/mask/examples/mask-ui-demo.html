--- title: Mask ---
> To use a mask you must declare the `data-av-mask` attribute and assign a predefined format or a mask definition.
#### Predefined formats include: * `data-av-mask="date"` which is equivalent to `99/99/9999` * `data-av-mask="phone"` which is equivalent to `(999) 999-9999` * `data-av-mask="SSN"` which is equivalent to `999-99-9999` #### Roll a custom mask definition by using: * `9` for a digit, * `a` for a letter * `*` for either a digit or a letter
